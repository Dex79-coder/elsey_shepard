1 Albert Young Shepard

Albert Young Shepard was born on 9 October 1845, in Rich Valley, Smyth County, Virginia, United States. His spouse was Mary Oregon Johnson, who was born on 26 March 1847 in Chatham Hill, Smyth County, Virginia. They were married on 22 January 1865 in Smyth County, Virginia; he was 19 and she was 17. Together they had at least 4 children. Mary Oregon Johnson died on 24 September 1931 in Oakley, Cassia County, Idaho, at the age of 84. Albert Young Shepard died on 16 March 1945 in Oakley, Cassia County, Idaho, at the age of 99. He was buried in Oakley Cemetery, Oakley, Cassia County, Idaho.
