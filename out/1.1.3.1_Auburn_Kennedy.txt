1.1.3.1 Auburn Kennedy
Auburn Kennedy was born on 18 December 1912, in Bradshaw, West Virginia, USA. Son of Elijah Kennedy and Lydia Belle Payne. His first spouse was Elizabeth May Grimm, who was born on 11 October 1914 in Pennsylvania, USA. They were married on 23 March 1935 in Brooke County, West Virginia, USA; she was 20 and he was 22. Together they had at least 4 children. Elizabeth May Grimm died on 27 April 1990, at the age of 75. Auburn Kennedy died on 11 May 1999 in Pennsylvania, USA, at the age of 86.
